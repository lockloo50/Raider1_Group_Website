    Value Proposition

    To Students

    1. We provide an environment with less Competition
    2. Easier methods of getting into communication with Companies
    3. Seeing more of the listings YOU want to see

    To Companies

    1. We make it easier to send out your decisions to students
    2. Get direct access to the students of the future
    3. Get you promote your listings above others with special paid advertisements

    How Our Site Works

    1. Students get recomended listings based off of provided information
    2. Students can watch video about the company/listing or read about the listing in its page
    3. Student's submit an application to a listing and can talk with a representative in a chat box
    4. Eventually, the companies will update you whether you were accepted or not via our sites Application Status feature
